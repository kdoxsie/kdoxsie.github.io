Raster Project


I had the idea for this Photoshop project months before class started after snapping this photo of my cat, Rue. I instantly thought she looked like a giant monster cat that had just taken over the city, so I decided to make her into a supervillian - Giganticat - in her very own movie poster. Photoshop was a difficult application to work with as it was not very intuitive, but I'm hoping to get better at it by taking the Photoshop course over the summer.

Giganticat may have destroyed the city, but will she get away with it...?
